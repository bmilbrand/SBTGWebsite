Taku

Taku is the original image messenger. It dynamically reads your message as you write it and serves up relevant images to compose your message out of. Our team developed the complex backend server that makes the platform possible. They say an image is worth a thousand words, in which case Taku is worth it's weight in gold. Taku is currently available on the Apple App Store.

MySpace

When Myspace was reaching it's peak and climbing fast, our lead developer was instrumental in developing the Backend that was necessary to handle the surge in traffic. The experience gathered from Myspace taught not only how to handle large success, but also what has the potential to cause failure.

TruFaced

TruFaced is a platform that discovers the "Social Truth" about the people, places, and things around you. Our team developed the native iOS app for the launch of the service. TruFaced is now available on the App Store.

Wit To Say

Wit to Say is an app that helps socially awkward people find clever things to say in common situations. Our team was instrumental in creating the Native iOS app and developing an interesting Backend that was able to work in the best way possible. Not to mention, the experience made us all more clever. Wit to Say will soon be available on the App Store.

Krave-N

Krave-N creates iPad-based POPPs (point-of-purchase platforms) for Restaurants and Food Trucks. Founded and developed by one of our team members, he created the platform and has been distributing it to restaurant and food truck owners who were in desperate need of a point of sale system that could keep up with demand. They loved it, and most have been loyal customers ever since.
